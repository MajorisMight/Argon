Raiden Shogun is the current Electro Archon of Inazuma and the god of Eternity. Her true name is Raiden Ei. She is a powerful and skilled swordswoman, and her pursuit of eternal perfection has led her to isolate herself from the world, ruling through a puppet. She is a central figure in the Inazuma story arc, known for her stoic demeanor and unwavering resolve.


**Best Weapons:**
*   **Engulfing Lightning:** Her signature weapon, providing Energy Recharge and increasing Attack based on Energy Recharge.
*   **Staff of Homa:** Excellent Crit Damage and HP bonus, good for increasing her personal damage.
*   **The Catch (R5):** A strong F2P (free-to-play) option, boosting Elemental Burst Damage and Crit Rate.

**Best Artifacts:**
*   **4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate:** This is her best-in-slot artifact set, significantly increasing Elemental Burst Damage based on Energy Recharge.
*   **2-piece Emblem of Severed Fate + 2-piece Noblesse Oblige:** A viable alternative for increased Energy Recharge and Elemental Burst Damage.
